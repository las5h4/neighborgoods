Who We Are
NeighborGoods

We are a team of developers who created this site as a recommendation and review service. We strive to promote small, minority, women and differently-abled led and owned businesses, which are often disenfranchised and overlooked. This site will guide the conscious consumer to buy at and support these businesses, which will help build stable financial networks for these groups in our communities.
